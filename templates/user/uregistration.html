Online Voting Benefits

Some Online Voting Benefits

  1. Easy and efficient — takes only a minute or two to vote
  2. Convenient — people can vote online when it suits them - there's no ballot to mail or meeting to attend
  3. Higher response rates — email reminders and online convenience boost participation for busy owners
  4. No supply costs — no paper ballots, no postage and no printing
  5. No equipment — 100% hosted and electronic
  6. Automated — no time or resources needed for manual hand counts
  7. Cost-effective — conduct multiple votes annually
  8. Quick and easy — full-service setup and management saves you time, so you can work on other projects
  9. Automatic vote tallying — no manual counts of paper ballots
  10. Accurate — no duplicate or invalidated ballots
  11. No waiting for results — real-time results, no waiting for ballots in the mail
  12. Print the results — print out the results and use it to help tally physical ballots if they exist.
  13. Private — our third-party service provides a layer of separation between the voting process and individuals involved
  14. Secure — no unsecured paper ballots
  15. Authentication — each vote is captured with a date and timestamp along with the voter's internet address
  16. 100% Green friendly — not a single tree is harmed in this easy to use electronic voting process!
